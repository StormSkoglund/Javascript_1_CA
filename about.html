ABOUT US

Game Hub began as a physical game store in Tulleby, Norway, in 1995.

We started out selling used retro arcade machines and board games. In 2013 we moved our business online and started to focus on video games for consoles.

Our team consists of five passionate nerds who grew up in front of flickering TV screens and handheld beep-beep machines.

If you have any questions or requests, you are more than welcome to contact us in the “contact” section on our homepage.

We are always happy to help you out!
